Happiness Score of the World in 2018

Happiness and it's possible factors

The world happiness report ranks 155 countries by their happiness levels and also provides many different attributes that contribute to this happiness score. It can be inferred from the map thta most Western and European countries have the higher happiness score whereas the African countries have the lowest.

Top 5 & Bottom 5 Countries by Happiness Score

Taking a sample of the top and bottom 5 countries ranked by happiness score, It can be seen that there is a dramatic difference. This difference can be attributed to a number of factors which will be discussed based on the following visualisation.

Correlation between Happiness Score and these factors

Scatterplot of Top and Bottom 10 Countries by Happiness Score. Investigating the GDP per capita vs Generosity in these countries. While the Top 10 happiest countries are grouped together based on GDP and Generosity with exception of Finland, it can be seen that Generosity and GDP per capita does not have a good correlation when applied to the bottom 10 countries by happiness.
